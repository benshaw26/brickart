§04 — The Studio

"Brickwork is patient, slow work. Every wall is a hundred small decisions — bond, mortar, jointing — made a thousand times over. That's what separates a house from something worth looking at."

Tony founded Brickart in 1998 with one principle: brickwork should be something you're proud of, not just something that holds a house up. Twenty-five years on, that approach has taken the team from local garden walls to architect-led self-builds and a feature on Channel 4's Grand Designs.

Every project is led personally. No subcontractors, no rushed handovers, no shortcuts on mortar mix or bond pattern. If Tony's name is on the quote, Tony's on the scaffold.

The studio is based in Chesham and covers the surrounding counties, taking on a carefully limited number of projects each year — the kind of work where getting it right matters more than getting it done quickly.
